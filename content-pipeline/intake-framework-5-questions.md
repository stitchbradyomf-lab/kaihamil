# Kai Hamil Intake Framework
## "The 5 Questions" — From Community Entry to Productized Service

**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 24, 2026  
**Status:** Concept → Implementation

---

## The 5 Questions

1. **"Are you solving the right problem?"**

2. **"How do you know if you're solving the right problem?"**

3. **"How do you know when the problem has been solved?"**

4. **"Are you spending time doing what you want to do? Or what you have to do?"**

5. **"Why 'must you' do what you 'have to' do? What are the impacts of not doing?"**

---

## The Funnel: Free → Paid

### Level 1: Community Entry (Free)
**Format:** Poll / Quiz on social channels
- LinkedIn poll: "Which question keeps you up at night?"
- Instagram story quiz with swipe-up to full assessment
- Substack post: "Pick one question and reply with your answer"

**Goal:** Engagement, email capture, identify pain patterns

---

### Level 2: Self-Assessment Tool (Free + Email Gate)
**Format:** Interactive web quiz
- Answer all 5 questions with guided reflection
- Receive personalized "Problem Diagnosis" 
- Get mapped to relevant Kai Hamil framework/content
- Optional: Join community/feed for accountability

**Questions could branch:**
- Q4 (Want vs. Have-to) → Lead to "Systems serve presence" content
- Q1 (Right problem) → Lead to "5 Filters" framework
- Q5 (Must vs. impacts) → Lead to "Agency through Automation" positioning

---

### Level 3: Problem Audit Service ($99-299)
**Format:** Async consulting product
- Customer answers 5 questions in depth (written or Loom)
- Kai/Stitch analyzes response through frameworks
- Deliverable: 1-page Problem Audit report
  - Problem diagnosis (are you solving the right one?)
  - Framework recommendation
  - 3 specific actions to take
  - Resource links (content, products, tools)

**Add-on options:**
- +$100: 30-min call to discuss audit
- +$200: Custom automation/system recommendations

---

### Level 4: Facilitation Engagement ($2,000+)
**Format:** Done-with-you service
- Deep-dive on organizational/systemic problems
- Custom framework development
- Implementation support

**Entry from:** Audit reveals complex, high-value problem

## Technical Implementation

### Phase 1: Simple Quiz (Week 1-2)
- HTML page with form
- Email capture to Substack/list
- Manual response/analysis initially

### Phase 2: Automated Scoring (Week 3-4)
- Branching logic based on answers
- Auto-generate framework recommendations
- Stripe integration for paid audit tier

### Phase 3: Community Integration (Month 2)
- Discord/Slack community access post-quiz
- Public "problem wall" (anonymized submissions)
- Peer accountability matching

## Content Pipeline

### One Question Per Week
**Week 1:** "Are you solving the right problem?"
- Short-form video (60 sec)
- LinkedIn post with poll
- Essay: "The Most Expensive Mistake"

**Week 2:** "How do you know?"
- Case study of framework validation
- Instagram carousel: "Validation signals"

**Week 3:** "How do you know it's solved?"
- Success criteria frameworks
- "Definition of done" for life problems

**Week 4:** "Want vs. Have-to"
- "Agency through Automation" deep-dive
- Personal story: Calendar Tetris liberation

**Week 5:** "Why must you?"
- Constraint examination
- "The costs of should"

**Week 6:** Full 5-Question Framework
- Compilation post
- Quiz launch
- "Problem Audit" service announcement

## Positioning

**Not:** A personality quiz ("What type of problem-solver are you?")
**Yes:** A diagnostic tool ("Where are you stuck and what's the path out?")

**Tone:** Curious, slightly uncomfortable, ultimately empowering
**Promise:** Clarity on what you're actually dealing with
**Outcome:** Mapped to specific Kai Hamil resources or custom solution
