## 7 Monetization Pathways (Ranked by Effort/Return)

### 1. **Digital Products** (Low Effort, Scalable)
**What:** Self-paced courses, templates, worksheets, Notion templates

**Pricing:**
- Notion templates: $19-49
- Mini-courses: $47-197
- Comprehensive course: $297-997

**Examples:**
- "System // Self OS" Notion template (you already planned this)
- "Resilience Audit" worksheet (System + Self versions)
- "Modularity Assessment" for life design

**Pros:** Build once, sell forever. No calendar dependency.
**Cons:** Requires audience/traffic. Commoditizes quickly.

---

### 2. **Certification/Licensing** (Medium Effort, Recurring)
**What:** Train others to use and teach your frameworks

**Pricing:**
- Certification program: $1,500-5,000
- Annual license renewal: $500-1,000
- "Certified System // Self Coach" designation

**Model (from Wardley Maps):**
- Core content FREE (builds awareness)
- Certification PAID (validates practitioners)
- Licensed facilitators pay annual fee

**Pros:** Network effects. Others sell for you.
**Cons:** Quality control. Need critical mass first.

---

### 3. **Productized Consulting** (Medium Effort, High Margin)
**What:** Fixed-scope, fixed-price engagements using your frameworks

**Offerings:**
| Package | Deliverable | Price |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| Resilience Audit | 2-week assessment + report | $2,500-5,000 |
| System // Self Strategy Day | Full-day workshop + action plan | $5,000-10,000 |
| Continuous Improvement Sprint | 30-day implementation | $7,500-15,000 |

**Pros:** Predictable revenue. Efficient delivery.
**Cons:** Still trades time for money (just more efficiently).

---

### 4. **Cohort-Based Course** (Medium Effort, Community)
**What:** Live group learning with accountability

**Structure:**
- 4-8 weeks
- Weekly live sessions (90 min)
- Async community
- 15-30 participants

**Pricing:** $500-2,000 per seat

**Example:** "The System // Self Intensive"
- Week 1-2: Assessment (which quadrant are you?)
- Week 3-4: System frameworks (Problem Solving, CI, Flow)
- Week 5-6: Self frameworks (Resilience, Modularity)
- Week 7-8: Bridge + implementation

**Pros:** Community creates retention. Higher completion than self-paced.
**Cons:** Calendar-dependent. Requires cohort minimums.

---

### 5. **Membership/Community** (Ongoing Effort, Recurring)
**What:** Ongoing access to frameworks, updates, community

**Pricing:** $29-99/month or $297-997/year

**Includes:**
- Framework library (all tools)
- Monthly live Q&A
- Private community
- Early access to new frameworks

**Pros:** Recurring revenue. Deep relationships.
**Cons:** Churn management. Constant content pressure.

---

### 6. **Speaking/Workshops** (Variable Effort, Credibility)
**What:** Keynotes, corporate workshops, conference talks

**Pricing:**
- Conference talk: $2,500-10,000
- Corporate half-day: $5,000-15,000
- Corporate full-day: $10,000-25,000

**Pros:** Builds authority. Leads to other opportunities.
**Cons:** Travel. One-time revenue. Feast/famine.

---

### 7. **B2B SaaS/Tool** (High Effort, Highest Scale)
**What:** Software that embeds your frameworks

**Examples:**
- Assessment tool that maps users on System // Self matrix
- Team dashboard showing collective problem-solver types
- AI coach that applies your frameworks

**Pricing:** $99-499/month per team

**Pros:** True scale. Recurring. Defensible.
**Cons:** Requires dev resources. Long build time.

## Key Insight

The most successful framework monetizers do this:

1. **Give away the framework** (builds audience, establishes authority)
2. **Sell the implementation** (courses, coaching, tools)
3. **License the practitioners** (certification creates evangelists)

Your frameworks are the bait. The service is the hook.
