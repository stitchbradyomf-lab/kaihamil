# AI User Taxonomy 2.0: Two-Layer Classification
## Comprehensive Framework for All AI Chatbot Users

**Date:** March 5, 2026  
**Context:** Expanding beyond problem-solving to include creativity, entertainment, and consumption

---

## The Problem with Current Framework

**Current (Double Diamond):**
- Assumes: User has a problem to solve or goal to achieve
- Covers: Explorer → Analyst → Designer → Builder
- Misses: Entertainment users, creative expression, casual exploration

**The Reality:**
People use AI chatbots for:
- Solving work problems (current coverage ✓)
- Learning and understanding (partial ✓)
- Creative expression and art (missing ✗)
- Entertainment and conversation (missing ✗)
- Emotional support and companionship (missing ✗)
- Pure experimentation and play (missing ✗)

---

## Layer 1: High-Level Usage Profile (The Filter)

### Two Primary Axes

**Axis 1: Purpose**
- **Practical** → Goal-oriented, productive, solving something
- **Creative** → Expressive, artistic, generative

**Axis 2: Mode**
- **Consumption** → Receiving, experiencing, understanding
- **Creation** → Generating, building, producing

### Quadrant 1: The Researcher (Practical + Consumption)
**"I use AI to understand things better."**

**Primary Use Cases:**
- Research and information gathering
- Learning complex topics
- Analyzing data/documents
- Decision support
- Fact-checking
- Summarization

**Subtypes:**
- **The Decoder** — Makes sense of complexity (technical docs, research papers)
- **The Investigator** — Deep research, connecting dots, building knowledge
- **The Student** — Structured learning, skill acquisition
- **The Advisor** — Seeks guidance on life decisions, not just facts

**To Double Diamond:** If they decide to ACT on what they learn → become Producers

---

### Quadrant 2: The Producer (Practical + Creation)
**"I use AI to get things done."**

**Primary Use Cases:**
- Building products/solutions
- Writing code/content
- Solving specific problems
- Automating workflows
- Project execution

**Current Assessment:** Double Diamond Framework
- **Explorer** — Diverges on problem space
- **Analyst** — Converges on problem understanding
- **Designer** — Diverges on solutions
- **Builder** — Converges on execution

**Entry Path:** Users who want to DO something with what AI helps them create

---

### Quadrant 3: The Wanderer (Creative + Consumption)
**"I use AI to explore and be surprised."**

**Primary Use Cases:**
- Casual conversation
- Entertainment and amusement
- Discovering new ideas/perspectives
- Roleplay and imagination
- "What if" exploration
- Emotional engagement

**Subtypes:**
- **The Flâneur** — Browsing ideas, aesthetics, concepts without goal
- **The Companion** — Conversation for connection, loneliness mitigation
- **The Gamer** — Play, puzzles, interactive fiction, roleplay
- **The Curator** — Collecting interesting AI outputs, building collections

**To The Artist:** If they start creating prompts with intention → become Artists

---

### Quadrant 4: The Artist (Creative + Creation)
**"I use AI to express myself and make things."**

**Primary Use Cases:**
- Generating visual art/images
- Writing poetry/stories
- Worldbuilding and character creation
- Music composition
- Experimental art forms

**Subtypes (Media-Based):**
- **The Visualist** — Image generation, visual concepts, aesthetics
- **The Wordsmith** — Poetry, prose, dialogue, narrative
- **The Worldbuilder** — Characters, settings, RPG content, lore
- **The Experimenter** — Weird prompts, testing AI limits, avant-garde

**Subtypes (Style-Based):**
- **The Craftsman** — Refining outputs, iterative improvement
- **The Generator** — High volume, exploring possibilities
- **The Collaborator** — AI as co-creator, back-and-forth dialogue

## Layer 2: Deep Assessment (Quadrant-Specific)

### For Researchers → "Understanding Style Assessment"
**Axes:** Breadth vs Depth × Speed vs Thoroughness

| Type | Characteristics |
|------|----------------|
| **Scanner** | Quick overview, surface level |
| **Driller** | Deep dive, one topic at a time |
| **Mapper** | Connecting ideas across domains |
| **Synthesizer** | Creating frameworks from learning |

---

### For Producers → "Problem-Solving Style Assessment" (Double Diamond)
**Axes:** Problem Space vs Solution Space × Diverge vs Converge

| Type | Quadrant | Characteristics |
|------|----------|-----------------|
| **Explorer** | Problem/Diverge | Sees many possible issues |
| **Analyst** | Problem/Converge | Narrows to root cause |
| **Designer** | Solution/Diverge | Generates many solutions |
| **Builder** | Solution/Converge | Executes chosen path |

---

### For Wanderers → "Exploration Style Assessment"
**Axes:** Internal vs External × Structured vs Freeform

| Type | Characteristics |
|------|----------------|
| **Daydreamer** | Internal, freeform (journaling, introspection) |
| **Seeker** | External, freeform (following curiosity) |
| **Player** | Internal, structured (puzzles, games) |
| **Flâneur** | External, structured (curated exploration) |

---

### For Artists → "Creative Medium Assessment"
**Axes:** Medium (Visual/Text/Audio) × Style (Craft/Experiment/Generate/Collaborate)

| Medium | Craftsperson | Experimenter | Generator | Collaborator |
|--------|--------------|--------------|-----------|--------------|
| **Visual** | Prompt engineer | Weird image explorer | Batch creator | Co-designer |
| **Text** | Poetry refiners | Genre-benders | Story factory | Writing partner |
| **World** | Lore builders | Reality-twisters | Character spam | RPG co-DM |
