## Summary

Users are complaining about performance declines in Claude AI, connected to recent changes Anthropic made to reduce the model's default "effort" level in order to economize on token processing costs.

## Key Details

- User complaints about Claude performance decline
- Anthropic quietly reduced default "effort" level
- Change made to economize on token processing
- Lack of transparency causing backlash
- Connected to compute cost pressures

## Implications

The AI quality vs. cost tension is real. Even Anthropic is feeling pressure to reduce compute costs, potentially at the expense of user experience. This creates opportunity for competitors and open-source alternatives.
