## Summary

Alex Finn has become a prominent voice in the AI builder community after creating Creator Buddy, an AI-powered content coaching tool that generates over $300,000 annually - built entirely without writing a single line of code himself.

## Key Details

- **Creator Buddy**: AI-powered platform analyzing social media posts to provide real-time improvement suggestions for content creators
- **Revenue**: $300,000+ per year with 80% profit margins
- **Timeline**: Built first app in August 2024 using Cursor, launched within 3 months
- **New Venture**: Launched "Ship/It Weekly" (formerly 1% Better) newsletter and Vibe Coding Academy
- **Mission**: Teaching others to build AI apps in 21 days, even without prior coding experience

## The Vibe Coding Academy

Alex recently launched the Vibe Coding Academy with a bold guarantee: students will build and launch their first AI app in 21 days. This reflects the emerging "vibe coding" trend where AI-assisted development enables non-programmers to create functional software.

## Implications

- **No-Code Evolution**: AI-assisted coding is blurring the line between "coders" and "non-coders"
- **Solo SaaS Viability**: $300K ARR as a one-person business demonstrates what's possible with AI tools
- **Content-to-Product Pipeline**: Alex leveraged his audience to bootstrap a successful product
