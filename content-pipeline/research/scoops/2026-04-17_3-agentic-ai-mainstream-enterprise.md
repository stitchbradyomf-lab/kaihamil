## Summary

New research and market data show agentic AI is rapidly moving from trial to mainstream enterprise adoption, with significant implications for infrastructure and business operations.

## Key Statistics

- **40% of business applications** will employ AI agents by end of 2026 (up from under 5% in 2025)
- **25% of companies** using generative AI launched agentic pilots in 2025
- **50% expected by 2027** - doubling adoption in just two years
- **94% of enterprises** raise concerns about agent sprawl (OutSystems research)
- **120+ tools** now compete across 11 categories (Taskade analysis)

## Enterprise Trends

- AI agents redefining security needs with autonomy and speed
- Microsoft transitioning from Copilot assistants to governance-first enterprise agents
- OutSystems introduced "Agentic Systems Engineering" for governed agentic systems
- Market fragmentation creating both opportunity and confusion
