## Summary

OpenAI launched GPT-5.4-Cyber on April 14, 2026, a specialized model fine-tuned for defensive cybersecurity work. Access is restricted to vetted security vendors, organizations, and researchers through their Trusted Access for Cyber (TAC) program.

## Key Details

- **Limited release**: Not available to general ChatGPT users
- **Vetting required**: Access tiers based on authentication as cybersecurity defenders
- **Already impactful**: Helped fix 3,000+ vulnerabilities in testing
- **More permissive design**: Allows capabilities restricted in standard models
- **$10M commitment**: OpenAI launched TAC program in February 2026

## Strategic Implications

- Signals OpenAI's response to Anthropic's cybersecurity focus
- Represents a shift toward "trusted access" models for powerful capabilities
- Positions OpenAI as a defender-first AI provider
