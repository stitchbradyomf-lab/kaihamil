### **DAY 2 - Saturday: LEGOLAND NEW YORK!**

**LEGOLAND NEW YORK OVERVIEW:**
- **Location:** Goshen, NY (about 60 miles north of NYC)
- **Ages:** Designed for ages 2-12 (perfect for 9-year-old!)
- **Theme Areas:** 7 themed lands
- **Best For:** LEGO lovers, families with elementary-age kids

**SAMPLE SCHEDULE:**
- **8:00am:** Breakfast at Dad's house
- **8:30am:** Depart Warwick for Legoland (35 min drive)
- **9:00am:** Arrive at park opening (check exact hours - typically 10am)
- **9:00am-9:30am:** Parking, entry, grab map
- **10:00am-1:00pm:** Morning attractions
  - Start with most popular rides first
  - Brick Street, Bricktopia, LEGO NINJAGO World
  - Height requirements vary by ride
- **1:00pm-2:00pm:** Lunch inside park or bring packed lunch
- **2:00pm-5:00pm:** Afternoon fun
  - LEGO Castle, LEGO City, Miniland
  - Live shows (check schedule)
  - Building experiences
- **5:00pm:** Depart Legoland

**IF STAYING FOR EVENING:**
- Some dates have extended hours or special events
- 4D Cinema experience

**Return Options:**
- **Option A:** Return to Dad's house for dinner & night 2
- **Option B:** Head to Hudson Valley hotel/Airbnb for night 2 (see below)

### **DAY 3 - Sunday: Hudson Valley Adventure + Return**

**Choose Your Adventure:**

---

#### **OPTION A: Beacon + Dia:Beacon (ART FOCUS)**
**Best for:** Creative kids who like modern art and exploring towns

- **9:00am:** Breakfast, checkout from Warwick or hotel
- **10:00am:** Drive to Beacon, NY (~45 min)
- **10:30am-1:00pm:** **Dia:Beacon**
  - Contemporary art in former Nabisco factory
  - HUGE installations kids can walk through
  - Sol LeWitt, Richard Serra, Andy Warhol
  - Children under 12 FREE
  - Adults: $20
  - Allow 2-3 hours
- **1:00pm-2:30pm:** Lunch on Main Street Beacon
  - **Homespun Foods** (casual, kid-friendly)
  - **Melzingah Tap House** (good for families)
- **2:30pm-4:00pm:** Explore Main Street
  - Hudson Beach Glass (watch glassblowing!)
  - Quirky shops, toy stores
  - Walk along Hudson River waterfront
- **4:00pm:** Depart for home
- **6:30pm:** Arrive Bucks County

---

#### **OPTION B: Storm King Art Center (OUTDOOR ADVENTURE)**
**Best for:** Kids who love running around, nature, BIG sculptures

- **9:00am:** Breakfast, checkout
- **10:00am:** Drive to Storm King (~40 min from Warwick)
- **10:30am-2:00pm:** **Storm King Art Center**
  - 500-acre outdoor sculpture park
  - Massive sculptures kids can interact with
  - Tram available but walking is part of the fun
  - Picnic areas - bring lunch or buy café food
  - Adults: $20, Students: $12, Under 5: FREE
- **2:30pm:** Late lunch nearby or start heading home
- **3:00pm:** Depart for home
- **5:30pm:** Arrive Bucks County

---

#### **OPTION C: Cold Spring (CHARMING SMALL TOWN)**
**Best for:** Shopping, ice cream, train enthusiasts

- **9:00am:** Breakfast, checkout
- **10:00am:** Drive to Cold Spring, NY (~50 min)
- **10:30am-1:00pm:** Explore Cold Spring
  - Main Street antique shops and boutiques
  - Ice cream at **Moo Moo's Creamery**
  - Hudson River waterfront park
  - **West Point Foundry Preserve** (short walking trail)
- **1:00pm:** Lunch in Cold Spring
  - **Hudson Hil's Café** (popular with families)
- **2:00pm-3:00pm:** Additional exploring or visit nearby:
  - **Breakneck Ridge** (too hard for young kids but view from below)
  - **Constitution Marsh** (boardwalk trail - easy, scenic)
- **3:30pm:** Depart for home
- **6:00pm:** Arrive Bucks County
