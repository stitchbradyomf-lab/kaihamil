# 🏞️ TRIP 3: ACADIA NATIONAL PARK FAMILY ROAD TRIP
## Summer 2026 (June-August)

---

## 📅 SUGGESTED DATES

### **RECOMMENDATION: Saturday, July 11 - Saturday, July 18, 2026**
**OR: Saturday, August 8 - Saturday, August 15, 2026**

**Why These Dates:**
- **July/August:** Peak summer weather (70-80°F, perfect for Maine)
- **Mid-month:** Avoids July 4th and Labor Day crowds/pricing
- **Saturday-Saturday:** Full week with travel days on weekends
- **Schools out:** Summer break for all ages
- **Park fully operational:** All facilities, roads, and programs open

**Alternative:** Late June (June 20-27) for slightly fewer crowds

---

## 🚗 ROUTE OPTIONS: Bucks County, PA to Bar Harbor, ME

**Total Distance:** ~580 miles  
**Drive Time:** ~10-11 hours (with stops: plan 2 days)

---

### **ROUTE OPTION 1: Coastal Route (RECOMMENDED)**
**Scenic, ocean views, charming towns**

**Day 1 - Saturday:**
- Bucks County, PA → Portland, ME
- Distance: ~420 miles
- Drive time: ~7 hours
- **Overnight:** Portland, ME

**Day 2 - Sunday:**
- Portland, ME → Bar Harbor, ME
- Distance: ~160 miles
- Drive time: ~3 hours (take Route 1 for scenery!)
- **Arrive:** Afternoon in Bar Harbor

**Highlights Along the Way:**
- Boston, MA (historic sites)
- Portsmouth, NH (charming waterfront)
- Ogunquit, ME (beautiful beaches)
- Portland, ME (foodie paradise, lighthouses)
- Camden, ME (picturesque harbor)

---

### **ROUTE OPTION 2: Inland Route (Faster)**
**Quicker, less scenic, more highway driving**

**Day 1 - Saturday:**
- Bucks County, PA → Concord, NH or Portland, ME
- Distance: ~350-420 miles
- Drive time: ~6-7 hours
- **Overnight:** Concord or Portland

**Day 2 - Sunday:**
- Continue to Bar Harbor via I-95
- Distance: ~200-250 miles
- Drive time: ~3.5-4 hours
- **Arrive:** Mid-afternoon in Bar Harbor

## 📍 DAY-BY-DAY ITINERARY

### **DAY 1 - Saturday: Travel to Portland, ME**
- **Morning:** Depart Bucks County
- **Afternoon/Evening:** Drive to Portland (~7 hours)
- **Night:** Overnight in Portland
  - **Dinner:** Eventide Oyster Co. or Duckfat

---

### **DAY 2 - Sunday: Arrival in Bar Harbor**
- **Morning:** Leisurely breakfast in Portland
- **Late Morning:** Drive coastal Route 1 to Bar Harbor (3 hours)
- **Afternoon:** 
  - Check into accommodation
  - Explore downtown Bar Harbor
  - Visit **Hulls Cove Visitor Center** (pick up park maps)
- **Evening:** Dinner in Bar Harbor
  - **Jordan's Restaurant** (casual, family-friendly)
  - **Geddy's** (harbor views)
- **Night:** Early bedtime for sunrise tomorrow!

---

### **DAY 3 - Monday: Cadillac Mountain Sunrise + Park Loop Road**

**CADILLAC MOUNTAIN SUNRISE (THE MUST-DO):**
- **4:30am:** Wake up
- **5:00am:** Drive to Cadillac Mountain
- **5:30am:** Sunrise over the Atlantic (first place in US to see sunrise!)
- **6:00am:** Return to hotel for breakfast and nap

**Alternative:** Do sunset instead (7:30-8:00pm in July)

**PARK LOOP ROAD TOUR:**
- 27-mile scenic drive through Acadia's highlights
- **Stops:** Sand Beach, Thunder Hole, Otter Cliff, Jordan Pond House
- Ocean Path trail connects Sand Beach to Thunder Hole (1.8 miles, easy)

**Evening:** Walk the **Bar Harbor Shore Path** at sunset

---

### **DAY 4 - Tuesday: Jordan Pond + Easy Hiking**

**JORDAN POND:**
- **Jordan Pond Path** - 3.3 mile loop around pond (mostly flat, kid-friendly)
- **Bubble Rock Trail** - Short hike to famous glacial erratic (0.5 mile)
- **Jordan Pond House** - Famous for popovers and tea

**AFTERNOON:**
- Easy kid-friendly hikes:
  - **Ship Harbor Trail** - 1.3 mile loop, coastal scenery
  - **Wonderland Trail** - 1.4 miles, great for tide pools
  - **Jessup Path** - Easy forest stroll

---

### **DAY 5 - Wednesday: Beach Day + Tide Pools**

**SAND BEACH:**
- Unique sand beach surrounded by mountains
- Water is COLD (55°F) but kids love playing in sand
- Restrooms and changing facilities

**THUNDER HOLE:**
- Waves crash into rock cavern (loud!)
- Best 2 hours before high tide
- Very popular - arrive early

**WONDERLAND TRAIL:**
- 1.4 mile easy loop
- Excellent tide pool exploring at low tide
- Bring water shoes!

---

### **DAY 6 - Thursday: Bike Riding + Bar Harbor**

**CARRIAGE ROADS:**
- 45 miles of gravel roads, no cars allowed!
- Rent bikes in Bar Harbor (~$25/day)
- Easy riding for kids, beautiful scenery
- Popular loops: Jordan Pond House (5 miles), Eagle Lake (6 miles)

**AFTERNOON:**
- Explore Bar Harbor
- Shopping on Main Street
- Ice cream at **Ben & Bill's**
- Maybe a whale watching tour (if kids are interested)

---

### **DAY 7 - Friday: Choose Your Adventure**

**Option A: More Hiking**
- Gorham Mountain Trail (moderate, great views)
- Beehive Trail (challenging, not for young kids)
- Great Head Trail (easy, dramatic coastal views)

**Option B: Water Activities**
- Kayaking in Frenchman Bay
- Paddleboarding at Jordan Pond
- Swimming at Echo Lake (warmer than ocean!)

**Option C: Relaxation Day**
- Sleep in
- Explore Bar Harbor slowly
- Beach time
- Early dinner, pack for departure

---

### **DAY 8 - Saturday: Departure**
- **Morning:** Breakfast, checkout
- **Late Morning:** Begin drive home
- **Overnight:** Stop in Portland, Boston area, or push through
- **Sunday:** Arrive home
