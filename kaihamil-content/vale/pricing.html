Common Questions

What does "dedicated AI instance" mean?
Your Valé runs on its own isolated server. Your data never mixes with other clients. It's like having your own private assistant that only knows your business.
What tools do I need?
Just your existing email, calendar, and file storage (Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.). We connect to what you already use. No new software to learn.
Is my data safe?
Yes. Your instance is completely isolated — separate server, separate database, separate everything. We use encrypted connections, and you can revoke access anytime.
What if the AI makes a mistake?
All actions are logged. Valé organizes and alerts — it doesn't make decisions for you. For anything important, it asks before acting. You're always in control.
What's the commitment?
3-month minimum, then month-to-month. 30-day cancellation notice. Your data is yours — we provide a full export if you leave.
How much of my time does setup take?
About 6-9 hours over 2-3 weeks. After that, you interact with your Valé as needed (usually a few minutes per day), plus monthly check-in calls.
