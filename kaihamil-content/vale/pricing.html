Questions

Why one-time instead of subscription?
Because you should own your tools, not rent them. The one-time fee covers my time to build and configure your Valé. After that, it's yours — running on infrastructure you control.
What are the ongoing costs?
Two things, both billed directly by providers (not by me): API tokens for AI usage (~$5-20/month depending on how much you use it) and VPS hosting (~$12-24/month for your dedicated server). That's it.
What if I need help after setup?
Optional annual service plans are available for ongoing support, config changes, and troubleshooting. But they're optional — your Valé works without them.
How long does setup take?
Personal: 1-2 weeks. Professional: 2-3 weeks. Team: 3-4 weeks. We work at your pace, with discovery calls and check-ins throughout.
Can I upgrade later?
Yes. Pay the difference between tiers plus a small reconfiguration fee. Going from Personal to Professional is common as needs grow.
