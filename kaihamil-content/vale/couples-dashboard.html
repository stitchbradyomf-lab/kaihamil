Marissa's View
Welcome, Marissa

This is Couples Valé — an operating system for us. Built because I wanted to be more present for you, for our kids, for our home.

As part of my new role, I decided to build rather than just study. Early months were figuring out pieces, getting them to work. By March, I saw what was possible — the infrastructure I'd built could become something real. A business, yes, but first: a system for us.

Everything I've built is to reduce the load we carry. The mental weight. The "who's doing what" fog. The coordination that drains us.

"I want to build technology systems that make people's lives easier. To automate as much as possible and reduce the cognitive and administrative load for working professionals and people in similar situations."

This is my proposal: Be the pilot customer with me. If Couples Valé works for us — if it actually helps us coordinate, connect, and carry less — then it will work for others.

Your experience shapes the product. Your needs drive what we build. You're not a bystander to my project — you're the reason it exists.

Let's build this together.
— Kyle
