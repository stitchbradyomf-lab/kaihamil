Content Site Design Concepts

Three approaches to transforming content.kaihamil.com from storage to destination.

🎯 Design Brief

  • Attention-grabbing: Splashy tagline and visual impact at first glance
  • Unique composition: Sections arranged around a central shape (not table/row)
  • Constellation concept: Root folders as icons against shape outline
  • Visual hierarchy: Clear prominence at top, guided eye movement
  • Clean aesthetic: Not busy — refined color palette (existing brown/amber/gold)
  • Same palette: Keep #faf6f1 cream, #3d2817 brown, #c9a227 gold

Concept 1: The Constellation

View Live →

Central hexagon with 8 section "stars" positioned around it like a constellation. SVG connecting lines create the network effect. Tags arranged at top, constellation in the center. Clean separation between hero and content.

Shape

Hexagon center with radial node layout

Movement

Eye flows from tagline → center hex → explore nodes

Feel

Networked, connected, systematic

Best For

Emphasizing interconnected frameworks

Concept 2: The Orbit

View Live →

Full-screen immersive experience with concentric orbit rings. Central dark core (System // Self) with sections orbiting at different distances. Massive tagline dominates the upper space. Stats strip at bottom.

Shape

Circular center with 3 orbital rings

Movement

Eye starts at dramatic tagline → explores orbital system

Feel

Cosmic, expansive, gravitational

Concept 3: The Bloom

View Live →

Diamond center with petal-like section cards radiating outward. Large bold tagline at top. Featured content strip for highlighted essays. Secondary grid for supporting sections. Most "site-like" of the three.

Shape

Diamond center with card-based "petals"

Movement

Tagline → diamond → primary petals → featured → secondary

Feel

Organic, structured, content-forward

Best For

Balancing visual impact with content discoverability

Concept 4: The Surfer

View Live →

Dynamic wave background with surfer riding the crest. Underwater depth = Operations, white peaks = Products, surfboard = Frameworks, surfer = Self. Extends "seeing the water" into "riding the wave."

Shape

SVG wave with depth gradient overlay

Movement

Surfer balances → explore peaks → dive to depths

Feel

Dynamic, active, intentional navigation

Best For

Reinforcing the core metaphor — moving from seeing to riding
