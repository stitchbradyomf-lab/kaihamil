Send a groove. Make a pal.

A pen-pal style vinyl sharing program that promotes experiencing and sharing analog activities with others. We track the journey of each record and enable conversations about the listening experience — bringing people together over shared non-screen time experiences.

How It Works

📦

Join & Receive

Pay $45 to become a Pal. Receive a welcome vinyl and 10 QR stickers to start your journey.

🎵

Listen & Share

Play the record. Log your favorite track, who you listened with, and a memory. Attach a sticker.

✉️

Send It Forward

Mail it to another Pal. The record travels. The story grows. Share 3, get 1 free.
