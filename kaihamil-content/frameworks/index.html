What is a Framework?

A framework is a thinking tool — a structured approach for navigating complexity. Unlike rigid systems, frameworks adapt to context while providing clarity. They help you see patterns, identify leverage points, and make better decisions faster.

Each framework here has been developed through research, tested in practice, and refined through iteration. Use them as starting points, not prescriptions.

How to use: Start with a diagnostic framework to understand your situation, then apply a decision framework to navigate your next move.
