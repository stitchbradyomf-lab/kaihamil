Decision Framework

The 5 Filters

A framework for deciding under uncertainty. Run any significant decision through these five lenses.

The Framework

When facing any significant decision, run it through these 5 filters. Each filter reveals a different dimension of the choice.

🧭 Filter 1: Directional

"Does this align with where I want to go?"

Tests strategic alignment. Will this decision move you toward your stated goals or away from them?

🎨 Filter 2: Preferential

"Do I actually want this, or do I just not want the alternative?"

Tests desire authenticity. Distinguishes between moving toward something vs. running away from something.

🧠 Filter 3: Epistemological

"How do I know what I think I know?"

Tests information quality. Are you working with facts, assumptions, or beliefs? What's the evidence?

⏰ Filter 4: Temporal

"How will I feel about this in 10 minutes? 10 months? 10 years?"

Tests time perspective. Reveals short-term vs. long-term tradeoffs and future regret potential.

🌊 Filter 5: Optionality

"Does this increase or decrease my future options?"

Tests reversibility and flexibility. Can you undo this? Does it open doors or close them?

🧪 Apply the Filters

Test your decision against all 5 filters. Be honest — the framework only works if you are.

Your Decision Assessment

How to Interpret Results

5 Passes: Green light. Proceed with confidence.

4 Passes, 1 Unclear: Yellow light. Gather more information on the unclear filter.

3 Passes or any Fails: Red light. Pause and reconsider. The cost of a wrong decision exceeds the cost of delay.

Remember: The 5 Filters don't give you the answer — they ensure you've considered the right questions.
