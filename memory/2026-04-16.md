## Daily Stitch Improvement - April 16, 2026

**What I Built:** Daily Intentions & Reflection System

A new integration that connects your morning brief with intentional daily planning and evening reflection. It's designed to keep your #1 priority (video reps) front and center while building a lightweight daily practice.

**Key Features:**
- **Morning Intentions** - Automatically integrated into your 5:30 AM brief. Shows video rep status, active priorities, and prompts you to set your daily intention
- **Evening Reflection** - Structured prompts to review your day, check video rep status, and log learnings
- **Weekly Summaries** - Track consistency across intentions, reflections, and video reps
- **Smart Integration** - Reads from DREAMS.md for morning insights, connects to Video Rep Tracker for unified progress tracking

**Commands:**
```bash
python3 scripts/daily-intentions.py morning      # Morning intentions
python3 scripts/daily-intentions.py evening      # Evening reflection
python3 scripts/daily-intentions.py intention "Ship a video today"
python3 scripts/daily-intentions.py reflect "Energy was 8/10, shipped!"
python3 scripts/daily-intentions.py weekly       # Weekly summary
python3 scripts/daily-intentions.py status       # Quick status
```

**Files Created:**
- `scripts/daily-intentions.py` - Main system
- `scripts/DAILY_INTENTIONS_README.md` - Documentation
- `memory/daily-intentions.json` - Your intentions (auto-created)
- `memory/daily-reflections.json` - Your reflections (auto-created)

**Why This Matters:**
Your morning brief now opens with your top priority instead of burying it. The system nudges without nagging — showing your streak, reminding you that "it just needs to *be*, not be perfect," and giving you a simple way to set and track intentions. It's built to reduce friction, not add it.
